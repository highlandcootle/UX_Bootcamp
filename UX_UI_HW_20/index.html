Re-homing

Thank you for contacting Austin Boxer Rescue. If you have a stray dog, you will need to go to the local shelter and give the owners a chance to reclaim.

Adoption Day

Austin Boxer Rescue hosts an Adoption Day Event every other weekend in Austin and the second Saturday of each month in San Antonio. Information about the event is located on the banner to the right. You are required to bring your dog to at least one of these events each month. Frequent attendance will give your dog additional exposure and increase the chances of a speedy adoption.

Frequently Asked Questions

So you want to re-home your dog and you are willing to keep him/her until placed? We ask that all owners keep their dog until we are able to match him/her with a new family.
We are often asked why someone should re-home their dog through Austin Boxer Rescue rather than use other avenues. There are many benefits to placing your dog through rescue if you can keep your dog until he/she is placed. For questions about the process that are not answered on this webpage, please email our Intake Coordinator at os@austinboxerrescue.com.
If you are seeking intake for your dog into rescue but cannot foster until placed, please send an email to os@austinboxerrescue.com explaining the circumstances in detail. Please understand that few dogs are accepted into rescue in this case. Shelter dogs at risk for euthanasia are considered first. Additionally, a monetary donation may be required to cover boarding expenses. You may also be required to have your dog fully vetted prior to acceptance into the Owner Surrender Program.
